# iPAS Support

If you need help, found a bug, or have a feature request, contact:

matthew@vertical-access.com

## Quick help
- **Can’t find a PDF?** Make sure the file is in Files/iCloud Drive or On My iPad and try again.
- **Annotations not showing?** Confirm you’re on the same page and zoom level where they were created.
- **App crashed or froze?** Send the steps you took + your iOS version + device model.

## Report a bug
When you email, include:
- iPhone/iPad model
- iOS version
- What you were doing right before the issue
- If possible: a screenshot or screen recording

## Privacy
This app stores your documents and annotations locally on your device unless you choose to share/export them.
